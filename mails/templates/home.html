Never forget to answer

rmd.io reminds you about unanswered E-Mails

How it works

Create reminders
Add reminders to your emails.
Send them to rmd.io.
3d@rmd.io means you get
reminded in 3 days.

Get reminded
After the due date expired,
rmd.io will send your mail
back to you to remind you
about it.

Manage your mails
Of course you can manage
your mails in the web GUI.
You're able to change the
due date and time easily.
